About Me

As a Seattleite born just South of the city, I developed a deep passion for creativity, technology, strategy, and storytelling as a young child. Even as a kid, I was a walking billboard, always wearing Mountain Dew and Dr. Pepper logos on my t-shirts without realizing I was already marketing.   

Marketing became my clear career path after I ended up in a marketing class in high school by chance. I quickly realized that marketing combined creativity, strategy, and technology in a way that felt completely natural to me. (And with Mark as my middle name, I figured it just made sense..)

Outside of marketing, I enjoy staying active through swimming, resistance training and hiking, and I love traveling to discover new and exciting places. In my downtime, I immerse myself in video games, shows, movies, and music, and I value spending quality time with friends and family.

I have a lot of new and interesting ideas for marketing that I would love to put to the test. I strongly desire to change the world of marketing for the better of all people around the world. I have found new ways that people could most effectively utilize Artificial Intelligence that will evolve the field of marketing for the better without replacing people.
